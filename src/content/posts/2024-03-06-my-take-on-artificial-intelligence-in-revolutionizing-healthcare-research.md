Research in health care means doing scientific investigation of various aspects of health such as causes and prevention of diseases, development of new medications and treatment procedures. It also includes ruling out old ineffective practices. This research is usually done by health care professionals, researchers and scientists and other experts who use continuous methods to develop new understanding. The ultimate goal of healthcare research is to improve health outcomes and quality of life for individuals and populations, as well as to inform about healthcare policies and practices.

 

**Why is health research important?**

Health research is really important for the existence of the human race as we have encountered new pandemic once every century. Most recently is covid 19 pandemic which not only demage our economy but our daily activites too. Health research can provide us with new trends and underlying causes as well. Just in the case covid 19 strains which kept changing it shape and the way they attack human organ is health research can help developing advance techinqiues to reduce the risk of these diseases. Health research not help to reduce the diseases risks but also increase the productivity of humans, which in turn affects the economies.

**Use of Artificial Intelligence in Health Research**



Artificial Intelligence is a field of computer science that involves the development of machines and algorithms to perform tasks usually done by humans, such as  learning, reasoning, predicting, and making decisions based on data through use of various algorithms. These days artificail intellegence is widely used in health sectors, which includes helping researchers develop treatments for diseases, developing medicine and improving patients outcomes and helping medical students. 

Artificial Intelligence  can be applied to health care tasks such as medicine discovery , patient diseases diagnosed at early stages, cancer treatment, medical Image analysis, and personalized medicine etc.\
 \
 Recently pharma companies are using artificial intelligence to speed up and smarten the work of clinical development. Artificial Intelligence can help drug making companies to produce life saving drugs and batch the to end users effectively and timely e.g. during covid 19 pandemic huge demands of vaccines which causes lots of headaches for health care administrators around the world with the help of artificial intelligence phrama companies can solve this problem effectively.\

 Development of drugs is highly complex, risky ,timely and it requires a substantial amount of capital and resources. First step of any drug development is the discovery of a drug. Researchers spend years discovering new drugs with the help of artificial intelligence. We can reduce this time.AI can reduce the number of possibilities that need to be explored through traditional means.\
 \
 Second step of drug development is pre-clinical trails in which drugs can be tested on animals to help researchers gather data for clinical trails. Preclinical trails usually include applying drugs to human cells and tissues to see whether a new drug is toxic to human cells or not.After going through these tough studies, the data is gathered, which can be huge for some time in petabytes. Artificial Intelligence can help identify the trends in Pre-Clinical Trails data which require dogs' age to analyze with the help of artificial intelligence. Researchers can quickly move to the next step by doing clinical trails on humans which are decided after getting positive results from pre-clinical research.\

Clinical Research is research consisting of different phases. It's extremely important to understand how this new drug interacts with humans. The goal of clinical research is to extract a drug which is safe and effective. There are a few things that can be evaluated during clinical research, such as

Clinical Trails usually consist of 4 different phases. Each phase has its own question which need to be answered either, postive or negative, upon which the scope of research during phases will be decided and this process goes on until all the questions are not answered. During phase 0, clinical a trails are done on small number of human around one and a half dozen. In this phase researcher uses extremely low amounts of drug provided that it does not poses any substantial threat of the lives of subjects.With the passage of time amount of drug is increased to achieve the desired results.

\
\
If drugs over react in a negative way, the researcher is forced to move back to pre-clinical research.   During phase one, number of subjects increased to a couple of dozen humans and ideal amounts of drug is administer to subjects and result is studied carefully.  Second phase is critical phase in clinical research of any new drug development. During this phase drug is tested for substantial people of people, around 600 to 800 who have living condition that need to addressed through this new drug.This phase spread on couple of months. researchers carefully and record any development sideeffects associated with this  newly developed drug. Most of drug failed at this stage which is highly costly for pharma companies which not only waste time but capital also.  After completing the second phase the clinical research travel to new phase three which requires a drug to be tested for thousands of peoples having diseases against which this drug is developed. This phase usually spreads over years. Researchers monitor and develop trends and records this data. Detailed study is required to complete this phase. Drug regulators around world make it mandatory to have phase 3 clinical trials to get approval of drugs.

 

The last phase of the clinical trial happened after approval form a drug regulator and it involved testing this newly developed drug on a mass of people for over years.

 

All of these phases require time and money, but the chances of failure are higher also. Artificial intelligence can help develop the pattern involved in each phase to see why the root causes of success or failure of each phase. **Will Artificial Intelligence Replace the Researchers and Scientists in Health Care?**\
 \
 Researchers and scientists are highly crafty individuals with skills like creativity, critical thinking and problem skills which are unlikely to be replaced with AI. Without research, it cannot be developed and used that way, it is used in industries day. Researchers can help supervise Ai while learning, on new data especially in health care, which is directly related to millions of human lives.Ai is a powerful tool to assist millions in various ways, such as analyzing large datasets, identifying patterns in clinical data which time-consuming and repetitive works in this nature which can be effectively managed by ai. However, AI is unlikely to do jobs such as formulating research questions and designing experiments. For example, while developing special theory of relativity einstein used thought experiments to develop theory and its mathematical equation were developed afterwards. This type of require human intelligence which will never replaced by artificial intelligence.\
 \
 Furthermore, Health Care Research requires ethical consideration that requires human judgement, such as ensuring the safety of patients and protecting patients' privacy.Ai can help humans in these tasks but ultimately, human researchers are responsible for making these ethical decisions.\
 \
In summary, Ai is a great tool for health care research but it is highly unlikely that it will replace human researchers and scientists in the near future. On, it will help them enhance their abilities and make them more productive, which is extremely beneficial to humanity.
